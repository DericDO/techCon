The Speakers

Dr. Fei-Fei Li

A leading expert in artificial intelligence, Dr. Fei-Fei Li is the Co-Director of the Stanford Institute for Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence (HAI). She could provide valuable insights into the latest developments in AI, its ethical implications, and how it is shaping the future of various industries.

Satya Nadella

As the CEO of Microsoft, Satya Nadella has played a key role in the company's transformation and its focus on cloud computing and artificial intelligence. He could speak about the future of technology, Microsoft's vision, and the role of tech in solving global challenges.

Elon Musk

Founder and CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, Elon Musk is known for his innovative approach to technology and his ambitious projects. He could share insights on the future of space exploration, renewable energy, and the impact of artificial intelligence.
