What you will find at TechCon

Embark on a journey of innovation and exploration at TechCon. Our conference brings together a dynamic blend of technology enthusiasts, thought leaders, and innovators. Discover the following key features:

Leaders

Engage with and learn from world-renowned leaders in the tech industry. TechCon provides a unique opportunity to meet and speak with influential figures shaping the future of technology.

Network

Build meaningful connections with future colleagues and collaborators. TechCon offers a platform for networking where you can connect with like-minded individuals passionate about technology and innovation.

Refreshments

Indulge in a diverse array of refreshments that complement the vibrant atmosphere of TechCon. Enjoy a culinary journey while exploring groundbreaking ideas and technologies.

Contacts

Expand your network by dropping your contacts at our dedicated space. Whether you're seeking partnerships, collaborations, or simply want to stay connected, TechCon is the perfect place to share your information.

Where will we be this year?

At TechCon, we've had the privilege of hosting our events in some remarkable venues, including the iconic: Percy Gee Building, the state-of-the-art Fielding Johnson Building, and the distinguished Sir Bob Burgess Building. These venues have witnessed the convergence of technology enthusiasts, innovators, and leaders, creating an atmosphere of collaboration and exploration. This year, we are excited to return to the esteemed Fielding Joohnson Building, where the rich history of our past events merges with the anticipation of new discoveries. Join us at this iconic location as we continue to push the boundaries of technology and celebrate the spirit of innovation.
